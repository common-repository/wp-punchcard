Easy social bookmarking for Wordpress with PunchCard

== Description ==

Easy social bookmarking for Wordpress with [PunchCard](http://noteslog.com/punchcard/) (the bundled version is 1.4):

1. PunchCard lets your readers submit your content to their [social bookmarking](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_bookmarking) sites with extreme simplicity
1. PunchCard shrinks 40 target sites in a very little space, so that you save real estate of your pages
1. PunchCard preserves the (40) target icons, while sharing a common minimal interface
1. PunchCard is fully configurable: submitted content, target sites, shapes and colors
1. You can add as many different PunchCards to your pages as you want
1. As a gadget, your readers will get their cards punched where they clicked: it's for recording that they already submitted that content to that target

= Examples =
*   `<div class="pc_STRIP"><div class="punchcard"> 
    <object> 
    <param name="punchcard" value='{ 
        title: "PunchCard is cool!" 
        , url: "http://noteslog.com/punchcard/" 
        , id : "PunchCard" 
    }'/> 
    </object> 
</div></div>`
*   `<div class="pc_MINI"><div class="punchcard"> 
    <object> 
    <param name="punchcard" value='{ 
        title: "WP PunchCard is coolest!" 
        , url: "http://noteslog.com/wp-punchcard/" 
        , id : "WP PunchCard" 
    }'/> 
    </object> 
</div></div>`

= Tips =
*   Use WP PunchCard with [Enzymes](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/enzymes/) to achieve greater flexibility


== Installation ==

1. Copy the wp-punchcard folder into your plugins folder
1. Activate WP PunchCard
